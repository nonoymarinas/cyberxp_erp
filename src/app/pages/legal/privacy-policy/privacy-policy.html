3. Our Privacy Roles

3.1 Personal Information Controller

Cyber Exploration generally acts as a Personal Information Controller when we determine why and how personal data is processed.

This may include personal data relating to:

  • website visitors;
  • customer representatives and authorized administrators;
  • prospective customers and sales contacts;
  • billing contacts and payors;
  • vendors, contractors, and business partners;
  • job applicants and personnel;
  • support requestors; and
  • users whose account, security, and platform-usage information we process for legitimate operational purposes.

3.2 Personal Information Processor

When a subscribing organization uploads, enters, transmits, or otherwise processes personal data through our ERP platform, the subscribing organization normally determines the purposes and permitted means of processing that data.

In that situation, the subscribing organization generally acts as the Personal Information Controller, and Cyber Exploration acts as its Personal Information Processor.

We process customer-controlled data only:

  • to provide and operate the contracted Services;
  • according to the customer’s documented instructions;
  • as described in the applicable subscription agreement or data processing agreement;
  • to protect the security and integrity of the Services; or
  • when required by applicable law.
